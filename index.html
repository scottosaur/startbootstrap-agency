Let's Get Married!

Details

Dress

We kindly suggest that you dress for a "cruise to cocktail" atmosphere. We want our guests to feel elegant but comfortable, keeping in mind we will be outdoors in early summer.

Dining

We will be serving a light meal of small plates inspired by our Chicago favorites followed by cake. Wine, beer, cider, and cocktails will be provided to toast our future together.

Accomodations

We do not have specific hotel reservations. There are numerous excellent options both near the river as well as throughout the city, and we would be happy to make suggestions if needed.

    Boarding Begins

    We will be boarding Chicago's First Lady promptly at 7:30pm. The dock is located along the Chicago Riverwalk, just east of Michigan Avenue. More details for locating the boat are available here.

    Cruise Departs

    Our cruise will take us along the river and out to Lake Michigan for gorgeous views of Chicago's architecture and skyline.

  • 8:15 PM

    Ceremony

    Our wedding will be held on the upper deck of the boat at Wolf Point, the fork of the north and south branches of the Chicago river.

  • 8:30 PM

    Dining & Celebration

    Following the ceremony, food will be served, music will be played, and we hope our guests will be able to enjoy both the company and the views.
