Location

Our wedding will be celebrated on the Chicago River aboard Chicago’s First Lady, a charming and elegant river cruise boat that docks on the Chicago Riverwalk near the Chicago Architecture Center.

The boarding area is easily accessed from the Riverwalk itself on the south bank of the river, just east of the Michigan Avenue bridge. There are stairs down to the Riverwalk at the northeast corner of Michigan Avenue and Wacker Drive that lead directly to the boarding area.

If you park in the Chicago Architecture Center’s attached garage (LAZ Parking Service Garage located at 111 E. Wacker Dr), there is an exit directly to the Riverwalk.

Further information for locating the boarding area is available directly on the Chicago’s First Lady Cruises website .
